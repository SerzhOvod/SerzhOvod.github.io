  • Concrete

    Concrete is a construction material composed of cement, fine aggregates (sand, crushed stone sand) and coarse aggregates mixed with water and chemical additives which hardens with certain time.

    Among a wide variety of construction services our company offers concrete work such as concrete pouring, concrete repair, concrete heating, heated driveways, stamp concrete, concrete foundation etc.

    It's also definitely well known that concrete can be casted in any shape, because it is a plastic material in fresh state. In modern construction various of shapes and sizes of forms or frameworks are used to provide different shapes (rectangular, circular, irregular etc).

  • Drain & plumbing

    For most of people, the terms like drainage and plumbing are the similar interchangeable words which are describing a some kind of engineering construction of water supply system or a network of pipes that help a home function properly.

    The main difference of these two terms is that plumbing supplies water (gas) into the home, when drainage carries water and wastewater away from the home.

    Everybody hope that would never deal with emergency drain or plumbing. But when it happen nobody knows what to do and whom to call.

    Our professionals are ready to cope with the task of any level of complexity in order to eliminate leaky pipes or backed drains. Moreover, we will consult and perform the work in a way that you will not remember them for many years.

  • Waterproofing

    Waterproofing is a complicated process of making structure, construction or any other object waterproof, or in in other words water-resistant. Similar objects may be used underwater to specified depth or in wet environments such as soil.

    Waterproof or water-resistant materials means resistance to penetration of water in its initial liquid state or under high pressure. Everyday in our life we come across such objects like pool, bathroom, basement, roof and others.

    Nowadays a huge variety of materials is existing to satisfy all needs the work of waterproofing to be done. Among them are glues, cements, chemical additives, polymers, bitumen, acrylic etc.
